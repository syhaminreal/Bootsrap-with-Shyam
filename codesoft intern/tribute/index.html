About the Show

Silicon Valley is an American comedy television series created by Mike Judge, John Altschuler, and Dave Krinsky. The show aired on HBO from 2014 to 2019 and follows a group of programmers trying to make it big in the high-stakes world of tech startups in Silicon Valley, California.

With clever satire, nerdy references, and iconic characters like Richard Hendricks, Erlich Bachman, and Gilfoyle, the show is both hilarious and brutally accurate about the startup culture.
